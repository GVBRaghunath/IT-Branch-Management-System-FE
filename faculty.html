About Us

The Department of Information Technology at Vignan's IIT has been active in teaching and research since its inception in 2002. Currently, about 15 committed faculty members are working in the department
IT-Department offers four-year under-graduate programme and two-year post graduate programme in the field of Information Technology.
The department contains efficient and eminent faculty & senior faculty mostly with B.Tech and M.Tech backgrounds.
